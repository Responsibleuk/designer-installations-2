Our

Process

  • Reimagining Bathrooms.

    The professionals at Designer Installations have the expertise to strip back your existing bathroom and reimagine it from the floor up — literally.

  • We can truly turn our hands to anything

    When necessary, we can reconstruct your flooring, relay your pipework and fortify your walls and ceiling to create a reliable, waterproof shell in which to house your new bathroom. Then comes the plumbing, plastering, carpentry, electrics, painting, decorating. We can truly turn our hands to anything — and we do so to the highest standard.

  • Fully insured and comes with a two-year guarantee.

    We are also fully qualified electricians, and can even install underfloor heating to create the ultimate luxury bathroom experience in your home. And whether we’re ripping out floorboards, stripping out your wonky old tiles or rewiring your electrics, you can be sure that we’ll leave your bathroom cleared, swept and secure at the end of each day. All our work is fully insured and comes with a two-year guarantee.

  • Health & Safety

    So whether you’ve got dogs, cats or kids running in and out, don’t worry — health and safety is our utmost priority.

Our Specialist

Services

Micro-Cement

Micro-cement is a cement-based micro-coating. Its adhesiveness and thin application mean it is incredibly versatile as a decorative material.
Learn More

Venetian Plaster

Venetian plaster is a lime-based decorative material whose roots reach far back into ancient times. The aesthetic is earthy and gentle, yet the iridescent veneer evokes a sense of depth and movement.
Learn More

Tadelakt Plaster

Tadelakt is a versatile plaster steeped in history, having been developed thousands of years ago in Yemen before spreading across the Arabic world and beyond. Tadelakt can be finished with either a shiny or matte veneer. The pigmentation is gently undulating, and no design can ever be replicated—meaning your bathroom will be utterly unique.
Learn More

Electrics

The builders at Designer Installations are specially trained in home electrics, meaning we can relay your wiring on the same day as crafting your ultimate luxury bathroom!
